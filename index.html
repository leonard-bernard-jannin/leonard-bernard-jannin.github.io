Profile

As a water resources expert with a PhD in Hydrology, I specialize in managing complex water systems through advanced modeling and data-driven decision support. With over 10 years of international project experience, I combine hydrological expertise, integrated modeling capabilities, and environmental knowledge to deliver practical solutions for sustainable water resource management.

I lead technical projects, coordinate multidisciplinary teams, and develop modeling frameworks to assess water availability, quality impacts, and management scenarios for watersheds, coastal waters, and aquaculture systems. Recent projects include oyster aquaculture capacity assessment in Hong Kong (2023-25), EU Water Framework Directive compliance in Denmark (2023), and integrated catchment-coastal modeling in Northern Ireland (2018-23).
